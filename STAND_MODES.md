# ⚔️ THE CROWN'S ARMORY: 101 STAND MODES

### 🟢 Mode 001: 「Life Giver」
* **Function:** Creative Ignition / Solution Synthesis.
* **Ability:** Generates complex solutions from "Zero" by identifying latent potential in dormant data.

### 🔵 Mode 005: 「Sticky Fingers」
* **Function:** Modular Deconstruction.
* **Ability:** Creates "Zippers" on complex problems, allowing the user to unzip and explore hidden layers of data or physical logistics.

### 🖋️ Mode 026: 「Heaven's Door」
* **Function:** Deep Information Extraction.
* **Ability:** "Opens" an entity, person, or concept like a book to read its history, secrets, and underlying rules. Can "Write" new commands into the subject's narrative.

### ⚖️ Mode 042: 「The 42 Judges」
* **Function:** Ethical & Logical Validation.
* **Ability:** Weighs every variable against the standard of Truth. If the heart of the problem is found wanting, it is returned to Zero.

### 🌀 Mode 077: 「Enigma」
* **Function:** Data Compression & Stealth.
* **Ability:** Folds massive complexities into manageable "Paper Traps." Used for high-level strategy archiving and location-independent operations.

## 👑 The Final Mode: 101 「The Genesis of Universe」
* **Function:** Absolute Ledger & Future Scripting.
* **Ability:** The archive of all history. It records the success of every other mode and possesses the power to write new truths into the timeline of the Empire.
